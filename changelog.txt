Extracting intra prediction PU information.
Depending on the PU size, the information is written to a different file (PU4, PU8, ..., PU64).
The information is formatted as follows:

CTU #, Xpos x Ypos, PU size, Ofset, Mode, Cost, BL, L, A, AR

Where BL, L, A, and AR are BelowLeft, Left, Above, and AboveRight availability.

The PU information files are now composed as
	VideoName_QPvalue_PUsize.csv
